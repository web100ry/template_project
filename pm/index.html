Checklist for Project Manager
Website development

    I. Creating a list of the technical requirements and website functionality.

    Its example structure:
    1. Terms which are used in the technical requirements
    2. General requirements
      1. Name of the site
      2. Names of the agreement parties: name of developer enterprise and name of client (user) of the site and their requisites
      3. List of documents on the basis of which the site is created
      4. Structure and scope of work
      5. The way of deliverable presentation
    3. Functionality and objectives of the site development
      1. Objectives of the site development
      2. Tasks which are solved with the help of the site
    4. Site and software requirements
      1. Software requirements for the site
      2. General requirements for the design and layout in a web page
      3. Requirements for the number and qualifications of the staff working on site
      4. Administration System Requirements
    5. Site structure
    6. Language versions of the site
    7. User Groups
    8. Website design
    9. Site Navigation
      1. Main navigation menu
      2. Additional site navigation
    10. Description of site pages
      1. Description of static pages
      2. Dynamic pages description
    11. Site functionality
    12. Site content
      1. Format of submitting materials for the site
    13. Additional information
    14. The control and work approval
    15. Requisites and signatures of the parties

II. Looking for executors:

    1. Freelance online marketplaces
    2. Search engine
    3. Google / Yandex maps
    4. Aquaintances
    5. Companies rating
    6. Data, who created the competitor`s site, other effective sites (most often information placed at the bottom of the site)

III. Holding the tender. The stage of information gathering about the candidates:

    1. Candidate's portfolio
    2. Price
    3. Project delivery time
    4. Technologies on which it is proposed to realize the project and why they should be used
    5. Payment terms and procedures
    6. Contracts
    7. Timeframe of project acceptance after its realization
    8. Standards of the company work (if any exist)
    9. Office address and location (if any exist)
    10. Pages in social media
    11. How will be conducted bug fixes after the project delivery
    12. Who will lead/manage the project
    13. Responsibility for errors
    14. The experience of SEO-promotion, online advertising
    15. In case of necessity how will be arranged the payment for project rework
    16. Further maintenance and support of the project, price, and conditions

IV. Choosing an expert. Positions to be checked:

    1. Company website
    2. Vacancies - are they looking for stuff
    3. Portfolio, quality, and expertise of works
    4. You should check the company and the director (www.crunchbase.com)
    5. To study and verify the terms of technical requirements with the agreement as well as the due dates for every block
    6. Conducting a job interview with people who will be involved or will manage the project, is there an understanding of your business processes
    7. Check whether the recommended technology is wide used

V. Recommendations for the organization of working process on the development stage:

    1. Weekly meetings, reports, testing
    2. Chat or channel for communications where you can list all agreements and decisions
    3. Permanent control of contractor and performed work
    4. Outside audit of works

VI. Site acceptance / portfolio testing

    1. Check the compliance with the terms of technical requirements
    2. Check how the site works on different devices, browsers
    3. Check of site speed loading, eliminating of bugs
      1. https://developers.google.com/speed/pagespeed/insights/
      2. https://tools.pingdom.com/
      3. https://gtmetrix.com/
    4. Mobile Friendly
      1. https://search.google.com/search-console/mobile-friendly
      2. https://testmysite.thinkwithgoogle.com/
    5. The code validity https://validator.w3.org/
    6. SEO tools (please refer to an SEO specialist)
